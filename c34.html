Les Misérables is a novel that tells the story of Jean Valjean’s struggles to escape his criminal past and provide for his adopted daughter, Cosette. Once released from prison, Valjean attempts to steal silver candlesticks from a priest. He is caught, but the priest gives him the candlesticks as a gift. Valjean becomes a wealthy mayor of a small town. He is forced to flee when Javert, a police inspector, finds him and grows suspicious.
